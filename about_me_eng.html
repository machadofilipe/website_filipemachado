Heyo!

My name is Filipe, I live in São Paulo, and I'm a designer with a degree from the University of the Arts London. I see design through sensitive lenses, with an interdisciplinary and research-based approach. I love learning about art history, photographing animals, drawing, and painting. I'm a Swiss Army knife designer, enthusiastic about new things and intimately curious, with a research-driven approach. I use digital tools like those in the Adobe suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, etc.) for branding and the like, but I also get my hands dirty with woodcut and other forms of multimedia.
