Heyo!

I'm Filipe, based in São Paulo and a designer who graduated from UAL. I see design through sensitive lenses, with a research-driven and interdisciplinary approach. I love researching art history, sticking my nose outside and taking pictures of animals, illustrating and painting whatever comes to mind. I tend to use digital mediums for branding and the like, but I love getting my hands dirty with physical multimedia and prints.
